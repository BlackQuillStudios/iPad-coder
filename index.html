Raising Cane's Sauce Recipe

This homemade version of Raising Cane's sauce is a creamy, tangy delight that's perfect for dipping chicken fingers or fries.

Follow the recipe below to make this tasty sauce at home. It's quick, easy, and only requires a few ingredients!

An image of homemade Raising Cane's sauce in a small bowl.

  • 1/2 cup of mayo
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
